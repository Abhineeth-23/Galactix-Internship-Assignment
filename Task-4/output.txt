1. **Phase 1 (2008-2012):** Introduced the core Avengers team (Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye).

3. **Phase 4 (2021-present):** Expands the universe with new characters, storylines, and teams, including the Fantastic Four, Doctor Strange, and the Eternals.

**Notable Films:**

1. **Iron Man (2008)**: Introduced Tony Stark/Iron Man (Robert Downey Jr.) and kicked off the MCU.
2. **The Avengers (2012)**: Brought together the core Avengers team for the first time.
3. **Avengers: Infinity War (2018)**: Showcased the culmination of the Infinity Saga.
4. **Avengers: Endgame (2019)**: Wrapped up the Infinity Saga and set the stage for Phase 4.

The Marvel Cinematic Universe has become a cultural phenomenon, with a vast array of characters, storylines, and interconnected events that have captivated audiences worldwide.
